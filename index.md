# Privacy Policy – Expense Tracker

**Last updated: 15 March 2026**

Thank you for using **Expense Tracker** ("the App").  
This Privacy Policy explains how information is collected, used, and protected when you use the App.

By using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

---

## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Personal Information

Expense Tracker does **not require user registration** and does not directly collect personally identifiable information such as:

- Name  
- Email address  
- Phone number  
- Home or work address  

You can use the App without creating an account.

---

### 1.2 Financial and Expense Data

All financial information entered into the App, including:

- Expenses  
- Budgets  
- Notes  
- Categories  
- Transaction history  

is stored **locally on your device only**.

This data is **not transmitted to our servers**, and we do not have access to it.

You are responsible for managing, backing up, or deleting your data.

If you uninstall the App or clear app storage, this data may be permanently deleted.

---

### 1.3 Usage and Analytics Information

The App may collect **anonymous usage information** to improve performance and user experience. This may include:

- Screen views  
- Feature interactions (such as adding an expense)  
- Session duration  
- Crash logs and diagnostics  
- Device model and OS version  

This data **cannot be used to personally identify you**.

---

### 1.4 Advertising Data (Google AdMob)

The App uses **Google AdMob**, a third-party advertising service provided by Google.

AdMob may collect information such as:

- Device advertising identifier  
- IP address  
- Approximate location  
- Device information  
- Ad interaction data  
- App usage signals  

This data helps:

- Deliver personalized or non-personalized ads  
- Measure advertising effectiveness  
- Prevent fraud and abuse  

Google may use cookies and similar technologies for ad serving.

You can learn more about how Google uses information from apps that use its services here:

https://policies.google.com/technologies/ads

Users can opt out of personalized advertising by adjusting their **device advertising settings**.
